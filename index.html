Dawn of Zombies

What Happened?

The government made everything turn to S*#T, and you wake up to a horrifc world fulled with zombies!!! You get your family and drive away as far as you can. Days past and now you and your family must find resources in order to survive!

How to Play?

You have 5 of each amount of clothing, food and weapons. In the input box, type in any number you want for your resources to go up!
